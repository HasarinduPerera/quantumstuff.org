Beginner

Basic Quantum Computing

Master the fundamentals of quantum computing through 8 interactive lessons. Learn about qubits, superposition, entanglement, and quantum gates. Perfect for beginners with no prior quantum knowledge required.

4 weeks
8 lessons
2,500+ learners
Interactive exercises

What You'll Learn

Understand qubits and how they differ from classical bits
Master superposition and quantum measurement
Learn quantum gates and how they transform qubits
Understand quantum entanglement and its power
Build and analyze quantum circuits
Explore real quantum computing applications

Course Lessons

1

Welcome to Quantum Computing

Discover why quantum computers matter, what problems they can solve, and get your first glimpse of quantum concepts. Learn about the exciting applications and your learning journey ahead.

15 min Interactive
2

Classical Computing Foundations

Understand how regular computers work with bits, logic gates, and circuits. Build a solid foundation that makes quantum concepts easier to grasp.

20 min Interactive
3

Qubits and Superposition

Discover the quantum bit and the magic of superposition. Learn how qubits can be in multiple states simultaneously and visualize them on the Bloch sphere.

25 min Interactive + 3D
4

The X Gate

Learn your first quantum gate—the quantum version of the NOT gate. Understand how the X gate flips qubits and see it in action on the Bloch sphere.

20 min Interactive
5

The Hadamard Gate

Master the superposition creator—quantum computing's secret weapon. Learn how the Hadamard gate creates equal superpositions and enables quantum algorithms.

25 min Interactive
6

Multiple Qubits

Explore exponential power: where quantum computing truly explodes! Understand how multiple qubits combine and why quantum computers scale exponentially.

30 min Interactive
7

The CNOT Gate

Learn about connecting qubits: the gateway to quantum magic. Master the controlled-NOT gate and understand how it creates quantum correlations.

25 min Interactive
8

Entanglement and Quantum Power

The grand finale: spooky action and real quantum algorithms! Discover entanglement, Bell states, and see how everything comes together in quantum computing.

35 min Interactive

Prerequisites

None! This course requires no prior knowledge of mathematics, physics, or computer science. If you can use a web browser, you can learn quantum computing!
